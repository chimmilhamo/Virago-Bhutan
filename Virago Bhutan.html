"Virago"

(n.) a strong, brave, or warlike woman; a woman who demonstrates exemplary and heroic qualities


Mission: TO BE STRONG, INDEPENDENT AND FREE.

We are a society of femists who want to educate the people of Bhutan about the true meaning of feminism, why it is important and help raise awareness about gender equality and woman empowerment. We dedicate this page to all the women out there who feel oppressed and the men who have been disrespected by toxic feminism.
